# Manage Contacts

In this guide you will learn how to manage SendGrid contacts on InBzar.

## Create a Contact

To create a new contact:

1. Go to the SendGrid → Contacts page.
2. Click on the **New Contact** button at the top right.

This opens a new form with different sections on SendGrid.

---

## Import Contacts

To import contacts:

1. Go to the SendGrid → Contacts page.
2. Click on the **Import Contacts** button at the top right.

---

## View Contact Details

To view contact details:

1. Go to the SendGrid → Contacts page.
2. Click on the contact you want to view.

---

## Edit a Contact

To edit a contact:

1. Go to the SendGrid → Contacts page.
2. Click on the contact you want to edit.

This opens a new form with different sections on SendGrid.

---

### Delete a Contact

:::warning

Deleting a contact can’t be undone.

:::

To delete a contact:

1. Go to the SendGrid → Contacts page.
2. Find the contact you want to delete in the Contacts section.
3. Click on the <UiIcon lightIcon="https://res.cloudinary.com/dza7lstvk/image/upload/v1667999308/Medusa%20Docs/UI%20Icons/1ordBC6_ssysel.png" darkIcon="https://res.cloudinary.com/dza7lstvk/image/upload/v1667999326/Medusa%20Docs/UI%20Icons/dSwWYBH_stzgoi.png" alt="three dots" /> icon.
4. Click on "Delete" from the dropdown.
