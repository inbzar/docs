# Manage Single Sends

In this guide you will learn how to manage SendGrid Single Sends on InBzar.

## Overview

SendGrid Single Send is a feature designed to simplify the process of sending one-time email campaigns, such as newsletters, announcements, or promotional offers. It provides a streamlined interface for creating and scheduling emails to be sent to a specific audience segment at a designated time. With Single Send, users can leverage SendGrid’s intuitive design tools to craft visually appealing emails that align with their brand's aesthetics and messaging. The platform supports personalization and dynamic content, enabling businesses to tailor emails to individual recipients, thereby enhancing engagement and effectiveness. Single Send also offers detailed analytics and reporting capabilities, allowing users to track open rates, click-through rates, and other key performance metrics. This data-driven approach helps businesses understand their audience better and refine their email strategies for future campaigns. By utilizing SendGrid Single Send, businesses can efficiently reach their target audience with timely and relevant communications, driving engagement and achieving marketing objectives.

## Create a Single Send

To create a new single send:

1. Go to the SendGrid → Single Sends page.
2. Click on the **New Single Send** button at the top right.

This opens a new form with different sections on SendGrid.

---

## View Single Send Details

To view single send details:

1. Go to the SendGrid → Single Sends page.
2. Click on the single send you want to view.

---

## Edit a Single Send

To edit a single send:

1. Go to the SendGrid → Single Sends page.
2. Click on the single send you want to edit.

This opens a new form with different sections on SendGrid.

---

### Delete a Single Send

:::warning

Deleting a single send can’t be undone.

:::

To delete a single send:

1. Go to the SendGrid → Single Sends page.
2. Find the single send you want to delete in the Single Sends section.
3. Click on the <UiIcon lightIcon="https://res.cloudinary.com/dza7lstvk/image/upload/v1667999308/Medusa%20Docs/UI%20Icons/1ordBC6_ssysel.png" darkIcon="https://res.cloudinary.com/dza7lstvk/image/upload/v1667999326/Medusa%20Docs/UI%20Icons/dSwWYBH_stzgoi.png" alt="three dots" /> icon.
4. Click on "Delete" from the dropdown.
