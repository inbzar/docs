# Manage Templates

In this guide you will learn how to manage SendGrid templates on InBzar.

## Overview
SendGrid Templates provide a versatile and user-friendly solution for designing, managing, and sending both transactional and marketing emails. With SendGrid's drag-and-drop editor and HTML customization options, users can create visually appealing and responsive emails that adapt seamlessly to various devices and screen sizes. The platform supports dynamic content, allowing for personalized emails that enhance engagement by tailoring messages to individual recipients. Reusable components, such as headers and footers, ensure consistent branding across multiple templates, streamlining the email creation process.

SendGrid also offers robust template management features, enabling users to categorize and search templates easily while collaborating with team members. Version control allows tracking changes and reverting to previous versions, ensuring quality and consistency. A/B testing capabilities help users experiment with different design and content variations to optimize engagement and conversion rates. Additionally, integration with SendGrid's Email API enables developers to send personalized and dynamic emails programmatically, leveraging predefined templates for efficient and scalable communication. Overall, SendGrid Templates empower businesses to create effective, personalized, and consistent email campaigns that enhance customer interaction and drive results.

## Create a Template

To create a new template:

1. Go to the SendGrid → Templates page.
2. Click on the **New Template** button at the top right.

This opens a new form with different sections on SendGrid.

---

## View Template Details

To view template details:

1. Go to the SendGrid → Templates page.
2. Click on the template you want to view.

---

## Edit a Template

To edit a template:

1. Go to the SendGrid → Templates page.
2. Click on the template you want to edit.

This opens a new form with different sections on SendGrid.

---

### Delete a Template

:::warning

Deleting a template can’t be undone.

:::

To delete a template:

1. Go to the SendGrid → Templates page.
2. Find the template you want to delete in the Templates section.
3. Click on the <UiIcon lightIcon="https://res.cloudinary.com/dza7lstvk/image/upload/v1667999308/Medusa%20Docs/UI%20Icons/1ordBC6_ssysel.png" darkIcon="https://res.cloudinary.com/dza7lstvk/image/upload/v1667999326/Medusa%20Docs/UI%20Icons/dSwWYBH_stzgoi.png" alt="three dots" /> icon.
4. Click on "Delete" from the dropdown.

---

## Template Reference

This section covers the template types supported by the InBzar and what variables you can expect in your dynamic template. You can use the variables to add details like order total or customer name.

:::note

You don’t have to create a template for every type in the reference. You can simply create templates for the type of emails you want to customize.

:::

:::tip

`default_template_data` in **example data** is what we used to build default templates.

:::
