# Manage Subscriptions

In this document, you will learn how to create and manage subscriptions for products. This includes creating new subscriptions, editing existing ones, and more.

Customers have two options for subscribing to products:

- On the checkout page, they can choose to purchase the product as a one-time purchase, or they can subscribe for recurring deliveries.
- Subscribe to Subscription Box

## Subscribe on Checkout

This is how it look like on standard checkout page, customers have two options Onetime and Subscribe.

## Create Subscribable Product

Under General Information Section you will see Subscription section. Here you have to to check the subscription box.

![Enable Subscription Box](/img/subscription-box.png)

::: Limitation

If subscription is enabled then customer can add only on product in the cart.

:::
