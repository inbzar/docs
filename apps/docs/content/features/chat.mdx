# Realtime Chat

The Customer Chat feature enables real-time communication between customers and store owners. Engage in instant conversations to address inquiries, resolve issues, and provide personalized support directly from your store's platform, enhancing the overall customer experience.

## Features

### Instant Messaging

Enable real-time communication between customers and store owners for quick responses and support.

### Direct Conversations

Facilitate one-on-one interactions to address customer inquiries and concerns directly.

### Conversation History

Access past chat interactions to review previous discussions and provide informed support.
